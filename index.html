Investment Meaning
WHAT COUNTS AS INVESTMENT?
Investment means money that is no longer just sitting idle or being consumed. It is money converted into assets or future earning power: stocks, funds, land, business assets, equipment, inventory, or scalable income capacity.

Savings protects you. Investment grows you.

Emergency Fund rule: the category named Emergency Fund represents the amount added this month. The app automatically builds the Total Emergency Fund across judged months.
